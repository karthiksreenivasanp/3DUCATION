# 🎓 3ducation — Learn Smarter, Together

> **3ducation** is an AI-powered cross-platform learning companion that transforms traditional education into an interactive, personalized, and accessible experience for students, teachers, and parents.

---

## 🚀 Overview

**3ducation** (pronounced *Education 3.0*) brings together the **three pillars of modern learning** —  
👩‍🏫 **Personalization**, 🤝 **Collaboration**, and 🧠 **Intelligence**

The app leverages **AI**, **speech and image recognition**, and **real-time analytics** to assist students in learning more effectively while helping teachers track and improve performance outcomes.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🧠 For Students
- 📚 **AI-Generated Notes** — Automatically transcribe and summarize lectures using voice and camera input.  
- 🔍 **Smart Search** — Instantly find topics across handwritten, typed, or spoken notes.  
- 📈 **Progress Tracker** — Visual dashboards showing strengths, weaknesses, and improvements.  
- 🤖 **AI Tutor Chatbot** — Ask questions, get explanations, and receive personalized learning tips in your native language.  
- 🎯 **Exam Predictor** — AI predicts important topics based on your previous answers and performance trends.

### 👩‍🏫 For Teachers
- 🗂️ **Lesson Planner** — Quickly generate structured lesson plans from textbook images.  
- 🧾 **Assignment Generator** — Create differentiated worksheets for students automatically.  
- 🗣️ **Voice Analytics** — Assess pronunciation and reading fluency during class activities.  

### 👨‍👩‍👧 For Parents
- 📅 **Attendance & Alerts** — Real-time updates on student presence and performance.  
- 📊 **Performance Reports** — Monthly and term-based summaries with progress graphs.
